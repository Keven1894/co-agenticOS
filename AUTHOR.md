### Background
Boyuan (Keven) Guan is a software engineer and AI collaboration researcher who believes in the transformative power of human-AI partnership in software development. With a passion for creating sustainable, ethical, and effective development cultures, Keven developed co-agenticOS as a response to the growing need for structured approaches to AI-involved software engineering.

### Vision
Keven's vision is to create a world where AI agents are true collaborators rather than mere tools, where software development becomes a continuous dialogue between human creativity and artificial intelligence, and where culture becomes the foundation for technical excellence.

### Philosophy
> "Culture is the new compiler. The quality of software is determined not just by the code we write, but by the culture of collaboration we create."

### Contributions
- **co-agenticOS Framework:** Complete cultural operating system for AI-involved development
- **Manifesto Development:** Philosophical foundation for human-AI collaboration
- **Workflow Design:** Practical implementation of co-agentic development loops
- **Community Building:** Open-source movement for ethical AI collaboration

### Recognition
Keven's work on co-agenticOS represents a new paradigm in software engineering, combining technical excellence with cultural vision to create sustainable development practices for the AI era.
